Reson8
WEB APP - DEVELOPING STAGEM

I built Reson8 during Hack Washington 2019 with a team of 4 people. Reson8 allows people with the inability to speak a means of communication through their hand gestures. A Myo band keeps track of these hand gestures and converts them into text. This text is then sent to Alexa for either communicating with other people (acting as a speaker) or communicating directly with Alexa (acting as a Home Automation Device).
