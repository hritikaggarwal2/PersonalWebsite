What's Around

ANDROID APP DESIGNER & DEVELOPER

I created a beta Android app (over-night at Esya Hacks '19) with Android Studio and Java that allows blind people to locate large objects like empty chairs, desks, and even mobile phones by scanning the room with their mobile phones. The tool uses TensorFlow Lite to scan the room and identify objects, and uses Android’s gyro and position sensors to track the motion and position of the user inside a room.
