Locality - Empowering Residents

2017

Locality is an Android App that empowers residents with the tools to make their neighbourhoods better places to reside. By relying on the principle of crowdsourcing, it gathers the most relevant information about local issues and facilitates communication between the people of the locality and the governing bodies to deliver faster, more efficient results to improve the lives of residents.

Space Settlement Model for ARSSDC

2016

Created a space settlement model for Asian Regional Space Settlement Design Competition with a small team of like-minded students. The model was a deliberate effort of 2 complete months and qualified for the Semi-finals. Even though we did not win in the semi-finals, this was a great experience and we all gained valuable knowledge with it.

Self-guided research on AI trends in India

2017

I wrote an article titled - “Artificial Intelligence In India – The Future Of Work” in the beginning of the year 2017. A.I has always captured my interest but the fact that many people doubted its success compelled me to write this article. So, I conducted a short survey with companies developing Artificial Intelligence Technology in India, to analyse the possible future that A.I could have on jobs and productivity. With the results of the survey, I wrote an article that was published on the online journal Youth Ki Awaaz.
