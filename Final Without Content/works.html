XINO - EXTREME INNOVATION

Club Website

XINO is the computer club of Delhi Public School Rohini which was founded in the year 2011. Since then it has gained huge success and recognition throughout Delhi-NCR and has become one of the most avaited events throughout the year.

I joined the club in 2010 and started honing my skills with the great support of my teachers and seniors. Soon I started participating in computer competitions both intra and inter-school. From being just a mere member of the club I extended my limits and became the core member and eventually in the year 2016 was appointed as the Secretary of the club.

In the year 2017, I was appointed as the President of the club and was acknowledged for my supreme skills at the subject. Being the President, I conducted a lot of workshops and created more opportunities for students to join the club. I, further, designed the club's website for the annual inter school fest along with other graphics.

All of my Graphic Designing Projects

Silicon Valley Poster

Vector Art

I created this silicon valley (popular T.V Series) poster for an interschool competition's preliminary event. This took me a lot of time to make as I had never created a true vector art. Being the first of its kind, it has some minor mistakes but a great start for a beginner. This single poster took me 2 hours (pretty long if I see it now), but all the efforts eventually paid off. The fact that I had never seen a single episode from the series did not hamper me from creating this project. This project was also exceptionally motivating as with this project I was able to understand the basic concepts of material design.
